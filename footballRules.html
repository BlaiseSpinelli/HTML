Play as the Marist Football Team!

In this game you will play offense for the Marist Football Team. You can choose your move from the three shown -- a dodge to side step a defender, a block to create a path for your teammate, or to really change the game, call an audible. Unfortunately, you do not know ahead of time what move the defense will choose. You each begin with 100 points. For each round of the battle, the winner takes 10 points from the loser. When either of you reaches 200 points, the game is over and one of you will lie defeated. The winner is determined by the list shown below. Push the button when you are ready to begin the game!
